Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Free AI Calorie Calculator is a mobile application that estimates calories and macro nutrients from food photos. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

Information We Process

The app may process food photos that you take with the camera or choose from your gallery. These photos may be sent to an AI analysis service to generate estimated food, calorie, protein, carbohydrate, fat, and portion information.

The app may also process app preferences such as selected language, ad status, and subscription status.

Food Photos and AI Analysis

Food photos are used to provide the analysis result. The app does not use food photos to identify you personally. AI analysis results are estimates and may be inaccurate.

The app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. For health, diet, or nutrition decisions, consult a qualified professional.

Advertising

The app may show ads, including rewarded ads. Ads may be provided by Google AdMob or similar advertising services. These services may process device information, advertising identifiers, approximate location, and ad interaction data to show ads, measure performance, and prevent abuse.

Google advertising policies are available at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Subscriptions

The app may offer a paid ad-free subscription in the future. Subscription purchases are handled by Google Play. Payment information is not stored directly by the app.

Sharing of Information

Information may be shared with service providers needed for the app to work, such as AI analysis providers, cloud hosting providers, advertising providers, and Google Play services. We do not sell your information.

Data Retention

The app is not designed to permanently store food photos. Some technical logs may be retained for limited periods by service providers for security, debugging, and service operation.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Permissions

The app may request camera, photo/gallery, and internet permissions. These permissions are used to capture or select food photos and connect to the analysis service.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the developer using the email address listed on the app's Google Play Store page.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page.
