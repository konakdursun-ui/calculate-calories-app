# Igne Atma - Play Store Listing Copy

## App Name
Igne Atma

## Short Description
Time your shot, pin the target, and clear each level.

## Full Description
Igne Atma is a timing-based mobile skill game built around quick decisions and clean shots. Launch your pins into the rotating target, avoid the pins already attached, and clear levels to earn coins.

Each level changes the target pattern and rotation speed. Earn coins by completing levels or watching rewarded ads, then unlock new pin skins and center themes.

Features:
- 500 levels
- Coins earned from levels and rewarded ads
- Unlockable needle, arrow, sword, dart, and spear skins
- Unlockable center themes such as soccer ball, basketball, tennis ball, and baseball
- Turkish and English language options
- Simple controls, fast rounds, and rising difficulty

Igne Atma is designed for quick sessions, satisfying progression, and increasingly challenging timing.
