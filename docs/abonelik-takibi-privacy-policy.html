Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Subscription Tracker ("the App") helps users manage subscriptions, recurring payments, bills, and monthly expenses in one place. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App may collect or store the following information:

  • Subscription names
  • Billing dates
  • Payment amounts
  • Categories
  • Reminder preferences
  • Currency and language settings
  • Premium subscription status

The information you enter is used only to provide the App's features.

Cloud Synchronization

If you enable cloud synchronization, your subscription data may be securely stored using cloud services linked to your Google account so it can be synchronized across your devices.

Notifications

The App may send local notifications to remind you about upcoming subscription renewals or bill payments. Notification permission is optional and can be disabled at any time from your device settings.

Advertising

The free version of the App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Advertising services may collect advertising identifiers, device information, approximate location, and usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads and measure advertising performance.

Google's advertising policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Premium Subscription

The App offers an optional Premium subscription that removes advertisements and unlocks additional features. Purchases are processed securely by Google Play Billing. The App does not collect or store your payment card information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data may only be shared with trusted third-party services necessary to operate the App, including:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing
  • Cloud synchronization providers (if enabled)

Data Retention

Your subscription information remains stored until you delete it or uninstall the App. If cloud synchronization is enabled, data may remain stored until removed from your account.

Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Internet access
  • Notification permission
  • Network state access

These permissions are required only to provide the App's functionality.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer using the email address listed on the Google Play Store page.
